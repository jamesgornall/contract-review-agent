PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

Contract Type: Fixed Deliverable

Client: Bristol City Council
Project: Citizen Digital Services Platform
Effective Date: June 1, 2024

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Development and deployment of unified digital platform for citizen services including online applications, payments, and service requests across all council departments.

2. DELIVERABLES

Phase 1 - Discovery and Design (8 weeks)
- Citizen needs assessment and user research
- Current state service mapping (120+ services)
- Digital accessibility audit (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Target architecture design
- Integration requirements specification
- Digital inclusion strategy

Phase 2 - Platform Build (16 weeks)
- Citizen portal development (responsive web)
- Mobile application (iOS and Android)
- Payment gateway integration
- Back-office workflow system
- Council service integrations (Planning, Waste, Benefits, etc.)
- Digital identity and verification system
- CMS for content management

Phase 3 - Testing and Launch (8 weeks)
- Accessibility testing and remediation
- User acceptance testing with citizen groups
- Load and performance testing
- Security testing and certification
- Staff training (200+ users)
- Phased service migration
- Launch and initial support
