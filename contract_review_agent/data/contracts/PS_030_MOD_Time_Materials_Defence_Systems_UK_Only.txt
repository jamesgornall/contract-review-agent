1. UK NATIONALITY AND SECURITY REQUIREMENTS

**CRITICAL NATIONAL SECURITY REQUIREMENT**

1.1 UK Nationals Only - Mandatory
Due to the classified nature of defence systems work, ALL personnel MUST be:

a) **British Citizenship Required**
   - British citizens by birth or naturalization
   - Hold valid UK passport
   - NO dual nationals from restricted countries (per MOD list)
   - NO foreign nationals under any circumstances
   - Renounced any previous foreign citizenship (if naturalized)

b) **Security Clearance Levels**
   - Developed Vetting (DV): 50% of team minimum
   - Security Check (SC): 100% of team minimum
   - Clearances must be current and maintained
   - Sponsor: Ministry of Defence
   - Regular re-vetting as per MOD schedule

c) **UK Residency Requirements**
   - Born in UK OR 10+ years continuous UK residency
   - Current UK address (verified annually)
   - No extended periods abroad (> 30 days/year requires disclosure)
   - Family members subject to security vetting
   - Financial ties to UK (mortgage, pension, etc.)

1.2 Prohibited Circumstances - Zero Tolerance
The following automatically DISQUALIFY resources:
- Any foreign citizenship (except Five Eyes: USA, Canada, Australia, NZ with MOD approval)
- Dual nationality with non-Five Eyes countries
- Close family members from restricted countries
- Financial interests in foreign defence contractors
- Residence abroad in past 10 years (extended periods)
- Previous access to foreign classified systems
- Employment by foreign governments/entities
- Undisclosed foreign travel

1.3 Ongoing Security Obligations
All cleared personnel must:
- Report all foreign contacts within 24 hours
- Declare overseas travel 30 days in advance
- Submit to regular security interviews
- Report changes in family circumstances
- Declare financial difficulties immediately
- Participate in annual security refresher training
- Submit to continuous vetting monitoring
- Report any security concerns or breaches

8. SECURITY CLEARANCE MANAGEMENT

8.1 Developed Vetting (DV)
Required for senior personnel. Includes:
- Extensive background investigation (10 years)
- Financial records examination
- Family members interviewed
- Former employers contacted
- References verified
- Psychological assessment
- Polygraph examination (for highest sensitivity roles)

Validity: 7 years (subject to annual review)
Renewal: Service Provider responsibility (£5,000-£8,000 per person)

8.2 Security Check (SC)
Required for all other personnel. Includes:
- Criminal records check
- Credit and financial check
- Employment verification (5 years)
- Security questionnaire
- UK residency verification (5 years minimum)

Validity: 10 years (subject to annual review)
Renewal: Service Provider responsibility (£2,000-£3,000 per person)

8.3 Clearance Lapses
If any clearance expires or is withdrawn:
- Immediate suspension from classified work
- MOD Security notified within 4 hours
- Replacement resource provided within 10 days
- No payment for suspended resource
