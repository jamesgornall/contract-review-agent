PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT
Contract Number: PS-2024-002
Contract Type: Time and Materials

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 1, 2024, between Qodea Professional Services Ltd ("Service Provider") and Retail Solutions PLC ("Client").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Service Provider shall provide ongoing software development and support services for Client's e-commerce platform modernization initiative. Services include but are not limited to:

- Application development and enhancement
- API development and integration
- Database optimization and management
- Performance tuning and monitoring
- Bug fixes and technical support
- Code reviews and quality assurance
- Technical documentation

Specific tasks to be agreed through weekly sprint planning sessions.

2. COMMERCIAL TERMS

Rate Card (Effective February 1, 2024):
- Principal Consultant: £1,200 per day
- Senior Consultant: £950 per day
- Consultant: £750 per day
- Junior Consultant: £550 per day

Minimum Commitment: 60 days per month across all resources
Maximum Cap: £180,000 per month without prior approval

Invoicing: Monthly in arrears
Payment Terms: 30 days net from invoice date
Overtime: Same day rates (no premium)
Weekend/Holiday Work: By prior agreement only, standard rates apply

3. RATE ADJUSTMENTS

Annual rate review: February 1st each year
Rate increases capped at: CPI + 2% or 5%, whichever is lower
Notice period for rate changes: 60 days

4. PROJECT DURATION

Contract Start Date: February 15, 2024
Initial Term: 12 months
Extension: Automatic renewal for successive 6-month periods unless either party provides 90 days written notice

5. WORKING LOCATION

Primary Location: Remote
On-site Requirements:
- Sprint planning: First Tuesday of each month at Client offices (Birmingham, UK)
- Quarterly business reviews: On-site at Client offices
- Ad-hoc on-site visits: By mutual agreement with 5 days notice

Working Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5:30 PM GMT (with 1-hour flexibility for standup meetings)

6. RESOURCE ALLOCATION

Current Team (subject to change based on Client needs):
- 2 x Senior Consultants (Full-time, 10 days each per week)
- 3 x Consultants (Full-time, 15 days per week total)
- 1 x Principal Consultant (Part-time, 2 days per week)

Estimated monthly utilization: 60-65 days
Estimated monthly spend: £50,000 - £60,000

7. RESOURCE CHANGES

Service Provider may rotate resources with:
- 10 days notice for individual contributors
- 30 days notice for Senior Consultants or above
- Replacement resources must have equivalent skills and experience
- Client approval required for Principal-level changes

8. TIMESHEETS AND APPROVAL

Consultants shall submit weekly timesheets via Client's approved system.
Client Project Manager to approve timesheets within 3 business days.
Disputed hours to be resolved within 5 business days or escalated.

Time Recording:
- Minimum billing increment: 0.25 days
- Travel time to Client site: Billable at 50% of day rate (max 0.25 days each way)
- Meeting attendance: Billable at full rate

9. EXPENSES

Reimbursable Expenses (with receipts):
- Travel to Client sites: Standard class rail or mileage at £0.45 per mile
- Accommodation: Up to £150 per night (when required)
- Subsistence: Up to £40 per day for on-site work
- Parking: Reasonable costs

Monthly expense claims submitted with invoices.
Client approval required for individual expenses over £500.

10. DELIVERABLES AND ACCEPTANCE

Work performed on time and materials basis with:
- Sprint deliverables demonstrated at end of each 2-week sprint
- Code commits reviewed through Client's standard peer review process
- No formal acceptance process for T&M work
- Quality standards defined in Client's development guidelines

11. CHANGE MANAGEMENT

Scope changes accommodated through sprint planning process.
No change request process required for T&M work.
Material scope increases may require additional resources or extended timeline.
Client priorities reviewed and adjusted bi-weekly.

12. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All work product created during billable hours becomes Client property immediately upon creation.

Service Provider retains ownership of:
- Pre-existing tools, frameworks, and libraries
- General-purpose utilities developed independently
- Skills, knowledge, and techniques

Service Provider grants Client perpetual license to use any reusable components in the work product.

13. SERVICE LEVELS

Response Times (During Working Hours):
- Production Critical Issues: 2 hours
- High Priority Issues: 4 hours
- Medium Priority: 1 business day
- Low Priority: 3 business days

Availability:
- Core team available Monday-Friday 9 AM - 5:30 PM
- Out-of-hours support: By prior arrangement with additional costs
- Planned absences: 2 weeks notice

14. PERFORMANCE METRICS

Monthly KPIs (tracked and reported):
- Sprint velocity and completion rate
- Code quality metrics (defect density, code coverage)
- Issue resolution time
- Customer satisfaction score
- Availability and responsiveness

Quarterly business reviews to assess performance and value delivery.

15. GOVERNANCE

Weekly Sprint Ceremonies:
- Sprint Planning: Mondays, 10 AM
- Daily Standup: 9:30 AM
- Sprint Review: Every other Friday, 2 PM
- Retrospective: Every other Friday, 3 PM

Monthly Steering Committee:
- First Tuesday of each month
- Review spending, progress, risks
- Plan upcoming priorities

16. CONFIDENTIALITY AND SECURITY

All consultants shall:
- Sign Client's NDA and security policies
- Complete Client's security awareness training
- Use only Client-approved tools and systems
- Protect Client customer data per GDPR requirements

Background checks: DBS check required for all consultants before starting.

17. TERMINATION

Notice Period:
- Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice
- For cause: 15 days notice with opportunity to cure

Upon Termination:
- Complete current sprint commitments
- Client pays for all time worked through termination date
- Knowledge transfer period: 10 days at standard rates
- Return all Client property and data

18. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

Liability Cap: £500,000 per incident
Insurance Requirements:
- Professional Indemnity: £5,000,000
- Public Liability: £5,000,000
- Employer's Liability: £10,000,000

19. COMPLIANCE

Service Provider shall ensure:
- Compliance with UK employment law
- IR35 compliance assessments where applicable
- GDPR and data protection compliance
- Security best practices (OWASP, ISO 27001)

Client responsible for:
- Platform compliance and regulatory requirements
- PCI-DSS compliance for payment processing
- Accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 AA)

20. GENERAL PROVISIONS

Governing Law: England and Wales
Dispute Resolution: Good faith negotiation, then mediation, then arbitration
Force Majeure: Standard exclusions for events beyond reasonable control
Entire Agreement: Supersedes all prior agreements
Amendments: Must be in writing and signed by both parties
