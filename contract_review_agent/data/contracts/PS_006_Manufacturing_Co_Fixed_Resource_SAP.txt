PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT
Contract Number: PS-2024-006
Contract Type: Fixed Resource (Staff Augmentation)

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2024, between Qodea Professional Services Ltd ("Service Provider") and British Manufacturing Co Ltd ("Client").

1. PROJECT SCOPE

Service Provider shall provide dedicated SAP consulting resources to support Client's S/4HANA transformation program. Resources will work as an integrated part of Client's project team under Client direction and management.

Project: SAP S/4HANA Implementation Support
Industry: Manufacturing
Duration: 18 months

2. RESOURCE ALLOCATION

Service Provider shall provide the following dedicated resources:

- 1 x SAP S/4HANA Solution Architect (100% allocation, full duration)
- 2 x SAP MM/PP Consultants (100% allocation, full duration)
- 1 x SAP FICO Consultant (100% allocation, months 4-18)
- 1 x SAP Basis Administrator (50% allocation, full duration)
- 1 x Integration Specialist (100% allocation, months 7-18)

Total FTE commitment: 4.5 - 6.5 FTE depending on phase

3. COMMERCIAL TERMS

Monthly Fixed Fee Structure:
- Months 1-3: £72,000/month (3.5 FTE)
- Months 4-6: £88,000/month (4.5 FTE)
- Months 7-18: £104,000/month (5.5 FTE)

Total Contract Value: £1,752,000

Payment Terms: Monthly in advance, net 30 days
Annual price review: March each year, CPI-linked increase (max 5%)

4. WORKING LOCATION AND ARRANGEMENTS

Primary Location: Hybrid - Birmingham Manufacturing Site
- Monday-Tuesday: On-site at Client premises (Birmingham, UK)
- Wednesday-Friday: Remote working
- Key milestones/go-live periods: Full week on-site as required

Client shall provide:
- Dedicated desk space and meeting rooms
- Access to SAP development, quality, and production systems
- VPN and remote access credentials
- Parking passes for on-site days
- Access to Client cafeteria and facilities

5. RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

Resource Substitution:
- Service Provider may substitute resources with Client approval (not to be unreasonably withheld)
- 4-week notice period for planned substitutions
- Substitute resource must have equivalent or better qualifications
- 2-week handover period for key resources

Notice Period for Resource Changes:
- Client may request resource changes with 30 days notice
- Service Provider shall provide replacement within 15 days
- No fee during replacement period if gap exceeds 5 business days

6. RESPONSIBILITIES

Service Provider Responsibilities:
- Provide qualified resources meeting defined skill requirements
- Ensure resources follow Client's working practices and policies
- Maintain resource performance and professional standards
- Provide backup resources for holidays and sick leave
- Conduct quarterly performance reviews with Client

Client Responsibilities:
- Provide day-to-day work direction and task assignment
- Ensure resources have necessary system access and tools
- Include resources in relevant project communications
- Provide timely feedback on resource performance
- Approve timesheets within 48 hours

7. WORKING HOURS AND AVAILABILITY

Standard Hours: 40 hours per week, Monday-Friday 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM
Flexibility required for:
- Go-live weekends (up to 4 per year)
- Monthly change windows (evening work)
- Emergency support calls

Overtime: Pre-approved overtime billed at 1.5x daily rate
On-call support during go-live: Included in monthly fee

8. DELIVERABLES AND ACCEPTANCE

While this is a resource-based contract, resources shall contribute to:
- SAP functional design documentation
- Configuration documentation
- Test scripts and test results
- Training materials for end users
- Cutover and migration plans
- Post-go-live support documentation

All deliverables subject to Client's standard review and approval processes.

9. KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS

Service Provider performance measured quarterly against:
- Resource availability: Minimum 95% (excluding approved leave)
- Resource retention: Maximum 1 unplanned change per resource
- Quality of work: Minimum "Meets Expectations" in Client reviews
- Responsiveness: 24-hour response to Client requests
- Compliance: Zero security or policy violations

Failure to meet KPIs may result in:
- First occurrence: Formal review and improvement plan
- Second occurrence: 10% fee reduction for following month
- Third occurrence: Client right to terminate with 30 days notice

All work product created by resources becomes Client property immediately upon creation. Service Provider retains no rights to SAP configurations, customizations, or client-specific documentation.

Security clearance: All resources must pass Client's background checks before commencing work.
