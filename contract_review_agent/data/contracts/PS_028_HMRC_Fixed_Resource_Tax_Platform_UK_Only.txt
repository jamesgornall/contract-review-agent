PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT
Contract Number: PS-2024-028
Contract Type: Fixed Resource
Classification: UK OFFICIAL

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of June 1, 2024, between Qodea Professional Services Ltd ("Service Provider") and Her Majesty's Revenue and Customs ("Client").

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Service Provider shall provide dedicated software development resources for the modernization of Client's tax assessment platform in compliance with UK Government Digital Service standards.

2. UK EMPLOYMENT REQUIREMENT - MANDATORY

**CRITICAL REQUIREMENT: ALL RESOURCES MUST BE UK-EMPLOYED**

2.1 Employment Status
ALL personnel assigned to this contract MUST be:
a) Direct employees of Service Provider incorporated and operating in the United Kingdom
b) Subject to UK PAYE (Pay As You Earn) tax system
c) Paying UK National Insurance contributions
d) Employed under UK employment law and contracts

2.2 Prohibited Arrangements
The following are EXPRESSLY PROHIBITED:
a) Offshore resources working remotely from outside the UK
b) Contractors or freelancers not on Service Provider's UK payroll
c) Third-party subcontractors without explicit written approval
d) Resources employed by overseas entities (including Service Provider's overseas subsidiaries)
e) Any form of IR35-non-compliant arrangements

2.3 Right to Work Verification
Before commencement, Service Provider MUST provide for EACH resource:
- Original Right to Work documentation (UK passport or valid visa)
- Proof of UK employment (employment contract, payslip)
- Evidence of UK PAYE and National Insurance enrollment
- Confirmation of UK residency status

Client reserves the right to audit compliance at any time.

2.4 Location Requirements
Resources must be:
- Physically located within the United Kingdom at all times during working hours
- Working from UK-based locations (office or home office in UK)
- Using UK-based network infrastructure and IP addresses
- NOT working while traveling outside the UK without prior written approval

2.5 Security Clearance
All resources require:
- Baseline Personnel Security Standard (BPSS) clearance minimum
- Security Check (SC) clearance for senior roles
- Continuous vetting throughout contract duration
- UK residency for minimum 5 years (for SC clearance)

3. RESOURCE ALLOCATION

Service Provider shall provide:
- 1 x Technical Lead (SC cleared, 100% FTE)
- 3 x Senior Developers (BPSS cleared, 100% FTE each)
- 2 x Developers (BPSS cleared, 100% FTE each)
- 1 x Test Engineer (BPSS cleared, 100% FTE)
- 1 x Business Analyst (BPSS cleared, 50% FTE)

Total: 7.5 FTE, all UK-employed
